# Quick Setup Guide

## Adding Member Photos

### Step 1: Prepare Your Images
1. Get each member's photo
2. Crop to a square (1:1 ratio)
3. Resize to 400x400 pixels (recommended)
4. Save as JPG or PNG format

### Step 2: Rename Files
Use the exact naming from the list below:

**Secretaries:**
- `elamugil_s.jpg`

**Additional Secretaries:**
- `senthil_kumar_t.jpg`
- `subash_r.jpg`
- `lakshitha_m_g.jpg`

**Joint Secretaries:**

**Members:**

- `logith_s.jpg`

### Step 3: Add to Folder
Simply drag and drop the renamed images into the `public/members/` folder.

### Step 4: Refresh Page
The images will automatically appear on the Office Bearers page!

## Features
- **Automatic Fallback**: If an image is missing or fails to load, a default icon will be shown
- **Responsive Design**: Images automatically resize for mobile devices
- **Hover Effects**: Photos have glowing cyan borders on hover
- **Professional Look**: Circular profile photos with modern cybersecurity theme

---

## Tips for Best Results
✅ Use professional headshots or clear face photos
✅ Keep backgrounds simple or uniform
✅ Ensure good lighting in photos
✅ Center the face in the frame
✅ Use high-quality images (not blurry)

❌ Avoid group photos
❌ Avoid heavily filtered or edited photos
❌ Avoid photos with busy backgrounds
